About

Badminton is a type of sport using rackets and badminton played by either two people opposing players (singles) or two opposing pairs (doubles), where the player's position is opposite in a square court divided by a net.

Players can collect points by hitting badminton with their racket so it can go over the top net and fall in the opponent's area. The rally shot stopped after the badminton fell on the floor, and the badminton can only be hit once per side before it passes over the net.

Badminton is an Olympic sport event with five competitive disciplines of men's and women's singles, doubles men and women, and mixed doubles, where each partner is a man and a woman. For the level high game, this sport requires excellent health care: players need aerobic stamina, agility, strength, speed, and accuracy. It is also a technical sport, which requires good body coordination, technical skills, and sophistication of racket use skills.
