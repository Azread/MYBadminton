The country's first representative won the world badminton championship

Oleh Sukhairi Thani - August 28, 2022

KUALA LUMPUR: The national men's doubles team, Aaron Chia-Soh Wooi Yik ended Malaysia's wait for their maiden title at the 27th edition of the World Badminton Championships held in Tokyo, Japan just now.

Aaron-Wooi Yik emerged as champions after defeating the opponent they met during the bronze medal match of the Tokyo 2020 Olympic Games from Indonesia, Mohammad Ahsan-Hendra Setiawan 21-19, 21-14 within 40 minutes.
